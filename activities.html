Watamu, Malindi

The shoreline in the area features white sand beaches and offshore coral formations arranged in different bays and beaches: Garoda Beach, Turtle Bay, Blue Lagoon Bay, Watamu Bay.

Mt.Longonot

A forest of small trees covers the crater floor, and small steam vents are found spaced around the walls of the crater. The mountain is home to various species of wildlife. Best suited for hiking and bird watching

Explore Longonot
Mt.Kenya

Mount Kenya is the second tallest mountain in Africa. The scenery surrounding this designated World Heritage Site is breath-taking. A variety of Animals and over 130 bird species.Also very much known for fly-fishing activity.

Explore Mount-Kenya.
Lake Turkana

Formerly known as lake Rudolf.It is the world's largest permanent desert lake and the world's largest alkaline lake.Best Known for fishing and generation of hydro-electric power. Our culture is diverse.
